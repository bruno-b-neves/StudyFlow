## 2026-07-02

### Decision #001

The project will follow a Monorepo architecture.

Reason:

Simplifies development for a single developer while keeping frontend, backend and documentation together.

---

### Decision #002

Version 1 will focus exclusively on language learning.

Reason:

Reducing the scope allows faster delivery and a more polished user experience.

---

### Decision #003

Artificial Intelligence will not be part of the MVP.

Reason:

The foundation (authentication, flashcards, study sessions and review engine) must be stable before introducing adaptive learning.
