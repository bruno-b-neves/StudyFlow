# StudyFlow

Version: 0.1.0

Status: Draft

Author: Bruno Neves

---

## Vision

StudyFlow is an intelligent language learning platform designed to maximize long-term knowledge retention through spaced repetition, multimedia resources, and adaptive learning.

Instead of simply presenting flashcards, StudyFlow continuously analyzes the learner's progress to personalize the study experience, helping users learn faster and retain knowledge more effectively.

The long-term vision is to become a complete AI-powered learning platform capable of understanding each student's strengths, weaknesses, study habits, and learning patterns.

---

## Problem Statement

Learning a new language is difficult because learners often:

- Forget previously learned vocabulary.
- Do not review content at the right time.
- Lack consistent study habits.
- Study without personalized feedback.
- Use multiple disconnected tools.

Current solutions either focus only on memorization or only on practice, without creating a personalized learning journey.

StudyFlow aims to solve this problem by combining spaced repetition, multimedia learning, and artificial intelligence into a single platform.

---

## Solution

StudyFlow provides an adaptive learning experience where every study session is personalized.

The platform combines:

- Flashcards
- Audio
- Images
- Spaced Repetition
- Learning Statistics
- Artificial Intelligence
- Progress Tracking

The system gradually understands how each user learns and adapts future study sessions accordingly.

---

## Target Audience

Version 1 focuses on people learning foreign languages, especially English learners.

Target users include:

- Beginners
- Intermediate learners
- Advanced learners
- Self-taught students
- Students preparing for language proficiency exams

Future versions may support additional learning domains such as programming, medicine, and university subjects.
